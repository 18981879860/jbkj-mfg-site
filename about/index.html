Who We Are

Jingbang Technology is a manufacturing-driven engineering partner focused on precision metal and plastic component production. Our expertise spans industrial fasteners, CNC machining, custom fabrication, and OEM component manufacturing for demanding mechanical applications.

By integrating advanced machining, cold heading, surface finishing, inspection, and assembly processes under one coordinated manufacturing system, we help customers simplify supply chains, reduce lead times, and maintain consistent product quality from prototype to production.

Core Capabilities

  • Precision CNC Milling & Turning
  • Cold Heading & Thread Rolling
  • Sheet Metal Fabrication
  • Custom Security Fasteners
  • Surface Finishing & Heat Treatment
  • Assembly & Inspection
