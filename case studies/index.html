Featured Projects

Highlighting some of our most impactful engineering solutions.

Custom Security Bolt System for Public Transit

Challenge: A municipal transit authority experienced persistent vandalism of copper components from trackside boxes secured with standard bolts.

Solution: Engineered a custom M10 Pin-in-Hex security bolt with a unique pin configuration and corresponding high-torque driver key.

Result: Reduced tampering incidents by over 95% in the pilot program.

Tags: Security Fasteners, Custom Manufacturing
Read Full Case Study →

5-Axis Machining of Aerospace Sensor Housing

Challenge: An aerospace client required a lightweight, single-piece housing with complex internal geometries. Material: 7075-T6 aluminum.

Solution: Utilized 5-axis CNC milling to machine the part from a solid billet in a single setup to maintain tight tolerances.

Result: Met wall thickness uniformity of ±0.05 mm and reduced part weight by 30%.

Tags: CNC Machining, Aerospace, Aluminum
Read Full Case Study →

Lightweight Titanium Lug Nuts for Racing Team

Challenge: A GT-class racing team sought to reduce unsprung mass to improve suspension response. OEM steel lug nuts were strong but heavy.

Solution: Designed and manufactured M14x1.5 open-ended titanium lug nuts from Grade 5 titanium.

Result: Reduced total weight of wheel fasteners by 45% while maintaining proof load above race specs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Expert answers about our case studies and project process.

How do we start a project with Jingbang Tech?
The process begins when you contact our team with your project details. We typically require a CAD model, a technical drawing, material specifications, and quantity requirements. We will review and provide a detailed quotation.
Can you work under a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)?
Yes, absolutely. We work with OEMs on proprietary designs every day. We are happy to review and sign your NDA before any confidential information is exchanged.
Do you only handle large production runs?
No. We support projects from rapid prototyping and one-off MRO parts to full-scale, multi-year production runs. Our flexible manufacturing setup allows us to be a valuable partner at every stage.
How are your case studies different from marketing material?
Our case studies are technical documents written by engineers, for engineers. We focus on the specific mechanical, material, and manufacturing challenges involved and detail the process and engineering logic used.
